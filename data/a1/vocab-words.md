## Other Words

These words are not nouns and do not have declensions:

* **nach** (after)
* **auch** (also)
* **bald** (soon)
* **bekommen** (to get)
* **schnell** (fast)
* **nett** (nice)
* **eigentlich** (actually)
* **dunn** (thin)
* **sachen** (things)
* **vielleicht** (maybe)
* **durstig** (thirsty)
* **traurig** (sad)
* **glücklich** (happy)
* **zuerst** (first)
* **stehen** (to stand)
* **beschäftigt** (busy)
* **verheiratet** (married)
* **ledig** (single)
* **geschieden** (divorced)
* **verlobt** (engaged)
* **kalt** (cold)
* **warm** (warm)
* **heiß** (hot)
* **früh** (early)
* **spät** (late)
* **gern** (gladly)
* **lieber** (preferably)
* **immer** (always)
* **nie** (never)
* **manchmal** (sometimes)
* **oft** (often)
* **selten** (seldom)
* **jetzt** (now)
* **heute** (today)
* **morgen** (tomorrow)
* **gestern** (yesterday)
* **übermorgen** (the day after tomorrow)
* **vorgestern** (the day before yesterday)
