## A1
These are some of the most fundamental adverbs for basic communication.

* **noch** (still)
* **auch** (also)
* **nur** (only)
* **sehr** (very)
* **jetzt** (now)
* **hier** (here)
* **dort** (there)
* **gern** (gladly)
* **zusammen** (together)
* **allein** (alone)
* **sofort** (immediately)
