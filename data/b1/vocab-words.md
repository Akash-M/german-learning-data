## Other Words

These words are not nouns and do not have declensions:

* **aktuell** (current/up to date)
* **behalten** (to hold/keep)
* **brav** (well-behaved)
* **eventuell** (possibly)
* **neugierig** (curious)
* **schließlich** (eventually)
* **tatsächlich** (actual)
* **aufgrund** (because of/due to)
* **Umstände** (circumstances)
